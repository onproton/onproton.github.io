About

I recently completed my Bachelor of Science in Information Technology through Colorado State University's online undergraduate program. My studies at CSU have included courses detailing network operations, systems/network security, hardware, database management (with SQL), and object oriented programming. Before that I spent three years studying biology at the University of Mary Washington, where I gained experience doing research, writing in a variety of styles, and analyzing data in an academic setting.

Over the years I have held a number of service and customer/client focused positions that have helped me develop strong communication and time management skills. I am comfortable and have experience working independently to meet goals or collaborating as part of a team, and I enjoy learning and exploring new ideas and technologies. In my spare time I like to tinker with old electronics and hardware, read about science and technology, practice writing code, engaging in political/technological discussions, and occasionally participate in citizen journalism.
